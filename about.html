About us

scorpion control project and research center is a community based initiative for prevention and protection from harmful animals such as scorpions,spiders,centipeds, snakes.
the scorpion sting is ranked second thread after malaria in the camp, So the scorpion control project and research center is a response to stings. We have taken great initiative that led the scopreck conduct. research that brought to light the scorpion species found in the camp and its surroundings we have made a catalog of scorpions of kakuma, a cartoon for public posters and a flyer containing basic information about keeping safety while living with these dangerous animals.
its main vision is to create and promote safe environment through awareness sessions creaate and initiate an interest for environmental literacy and natural science among the youth.

Objectives

To reach as many people as possible and imform them about the insects of kakuma and how to prevent scorpion stings
